<p><p>The baking enzymes market includes various types, notably carbohydrases, proteases, and lipases, each playing a critical role in dough production and bread making. Carbohydrases break down carbohydrates, enhancing fermentation and improving dough viscosity. Proteases facilitate protein breakdown, leading to better dough stability and gluten development. Lipases contribute to the emulsification and aeration of fats, enhancing texture and freshness. Together, these enzymes optimize baking processes, improve product quality, and extend shelf life, catering to the growing demand for high-quality baked goods.</p></p>
