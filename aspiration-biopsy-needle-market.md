<p><ul><li>Fine Needle Aspiration Biopsy (FNAB)</li><li>Core Needle Biopsy (CNB)</li><li>Vacuum Assisted Biopsy (VAB)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aspiration and biopsy needle market comprises three primary types: Fine Needle Aspiration Biopsy (FNAB), which uses thin needles to extract small tissue samples for cytological analysis; Core Needle Biopsy (CNB), employing larger needles to obtain cylindrical tissue samples, providing more detailed histological information; and Vacuum Assisted Biopsy (VAB), a technique that uses suction to enhance tissue acquisition, allowing for larger samples with less invasiveness. Each type varies in application, precision, and tissue sample size, catering to specific diagnostic needs.</p></p>
