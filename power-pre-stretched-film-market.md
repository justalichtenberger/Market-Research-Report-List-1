<p><p>The Power Pre-Stretched Film market is categorized into two main types: coreless and cored film. Coreless Power Pre-Stretched Film is designed to be used without a central core, allowing for easy disposal and reduced waste. This type is often lighter and more flexible. In contrast, Cored Power Pre-Stretched Film is wound around a central core, providing stability during handling and storage. Each type serves specific packaging needs, with coreless film favored for its eco-friendliness and cored film for its durability.</p></p>
