<p><strong>The Uterine Contraction Monitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Uterine Contraction Monitors</li><li>External Uterine Contraction Monitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Uterine contraction monitors are essential tools in obstetrics to assess labor progression. Internal uterine contraction monitors involve the placement of a catheter within the uterus, providing accurate measurements of contraction strength and timing directly, ensuring precise data. In contrast, external uterine contraction monitors are non-invasive devices placed on the abdomen, using sensors to detect contractions through the abdominal wall. Both types are crucial for monitoring fetal health and guiding labor management, catering to different clinical needs and patient conditions.</p></p>
