<p><p>An ultrasonic flow sensor is a device that measures the flow rate of liquids or gases by using ultrasonics, with sound waves to determine the velocity of the fluid. These sensors can be categorized into two types: transit-time and Doppler sensors. They are widely used in various industries such as water management, oil and gas, HVAC, and chemical processing due to their non-intrusive nature, accuracy, and ability to handle a variety of fluids.</p><p>The ultrasonic flow sensor market is experiencing significant growth, attributed to increasing demand for efficient water management systems, advancements in industrial automation, and the rising need for accurate measurement solutions in various applications.
