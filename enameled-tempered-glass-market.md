<p><p>The enameled tempered glass market is categorized primarily into two types: nontransparent enameled tempered glass and semitransparent enameled tempered glass. Nontransparent enameled tempered glass offers complete opacity, making it ideal for applications requiring privacy and aesthetic appeal, such as building facades and partitions. Conversely, semitransparent enameled tempered glass allows partial light transmission while maintaining visual obscurity, suitable for interior design, decorative features, and architectural elements. Both types are valued for their durability, thermal resistance, and versatility in design.</p></p>
