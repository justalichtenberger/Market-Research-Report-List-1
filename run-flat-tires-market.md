<p><strong>The Run Flat Tires Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-Supporting</li><li>Support Ring System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Run-flat tires are designed to maintain functionality even after puncture or loss of pressure. There are two main types in the market: self-supporting and support ring systems. Self-supporting tires have reinforced sidewalls that allow them to bear the weight of the vehicle temporarily, enabling continued travel at reduced speeds. In contrast, support ring systems utilize an internal support ring that helps maintain tire shape and stability when deflated, allowing for extended mobility without compromising performance or safety.</p></p>
