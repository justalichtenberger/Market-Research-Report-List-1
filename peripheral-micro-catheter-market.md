<p><p>The peripheral micro catheter market involves specialized devices used for minimally invasive procedures in vascular access. Two primary types include coils and spherical embolics, which are used to occlude blood vessels during treatments. Coils are wire-like structures that provide targeted embolization, while spherical embolics are round particles that ensure uniform vascular obstruction. The guided wires segment complements these products by aiding in the navigation and precise placement of catheters during interventions, enhancing overall procedural efficiency and safety.</p></p>
