<p><strong>The Three Phase Distribution Boards Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Main Distribution Boards</li><li>Emergency Distribution Boards</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Three phase distribution boards are crucial for managing electrical power in commercial and industrial settings. Main Distribution Boards (MDBs) distribute electricity from the utility supply to various circuits within a facility, ensuring effective load management. Emergency Distribution Boards (EDBs) provide power in critical situations, such as power outages, by switching to backup systems like generators or UPS. Both types enhance safety and reliability, with MDBs focusing on regular operations and EDBs prioritizing support during emergencies, ensuring continuous power supply.</p></p>
