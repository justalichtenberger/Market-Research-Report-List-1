<p><p>The tractor rear-view camera market comprises two main types: wired and wireless rear-view cameras. Wired rear-view cameras connect directly to the tractor’s electrical system, offering reliable signal transmission and often better image quality. In contrast, wireless rear-view cameras use radio frequencies to transmit video signals, providing easier installation and greater flexibility, as they eliminate the need for extensive wiring. Both types enhance safety and visibility, allowing operators to navigate and maneuver tractors more effectively in various environments.</p></p>
