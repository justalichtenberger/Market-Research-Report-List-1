<p><p>The polyurethane O-ring seals market is divided into two primary types: non-reinforced seals and fabric-reinforced seals. Non-reinforced seals are made solely from polyurethane, offering flexibility and durability for standard applications. In contrast, fabric-reinforced seals incorporate layers of fabric, enhancing strength, resistance to wear, and thermal stability, making them suitable for high-pressure and extreme conditions. Both types serve critical roles in various industries, providing reliable sealing solutions in automotive, aerospace, and industrial applications.</p></p>
